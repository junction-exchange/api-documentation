## Junction Exchange

New to crypto or advanced users? We got you covered\! We built Junction Exchange to faciliate your access to DeFi. From viewing your whole portfolio to manage it, buying your favorite crypto or exchanging it to another - we provide a global interface to explore all crypto-assets on all blockchains.

Connect your favorite wallet(s) or your favorite ecosystem(s), or simply connect with using our social integration using either your X, Facebook or Gmail account\!
