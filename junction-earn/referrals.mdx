The referral program created by Junction is a global layer of incentivisation on participation. Each addresses is linked to a unique code and can be linked to another one to benefits the program.

## Earn with Referrals

To start earning, you first need to get into our Junction Exchange - Earn, and connect your wallet

## Discounts

All addresses that referred will get a direct **10% discount** on their **trading fees**. The discount will be applied directly and automatically, working on all ecosystems/blockchains and for all transactionnals features (currently only swaps are considered in our earn program).

Learn more about [Junction Fees](junction-exchange/platform-fee).
