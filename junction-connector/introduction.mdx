Designed for scalability and flexibility across all ecosystems, Junction Connector allows users to connect with any wallet - or even multiple wallets simultaneously - to manage different accounts with ease.

From social logins to native support for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Thorchain, Cosmos, and more, Junction Connector lets users from any community seamlessly access DeFi.
